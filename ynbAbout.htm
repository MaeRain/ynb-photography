Hello,

My name is Yasmine Baroody. I’m a freelance photographer based out of Media, Pa. I’ve lived in the area all my life, and as long as I can remember, photography has always been something I truly enjoy. The ability to capture and preserve the beauty of a given moment is one of the many reasons I love what I do, and I want to continue to share and enjoy all of the wonder the world has to offer.

Equipped with my Nikon, I specialize in capturing nature, portraits, engagements, weddings, and other events. From there, I use a variety of editing tools to retouch pictures, to ensure the final product is perfect in every way.

If you’d like to inquire about my work, please contact me. I’d love to work with you!
